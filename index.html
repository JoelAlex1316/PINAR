About Us

Company History

Founded in 2000 E.C in Addis Ababa, our factory began as a small, family-operated soap workshop, renowned for its high-quality, handcrafted products. Over the decades, we evolved with the changing times, expanding into detergents in the 2005s/2007s to meet the growing demand for more efficient cleaning solutions. This transition marked a significant milestone, leading to growth beyond local markets and the development of innovative, eco-friendly products.


Today, our factory is a testament to this enduring legacy, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver a range of products that are both effective and environmentally responsible.
